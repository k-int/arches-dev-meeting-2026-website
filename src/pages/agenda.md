**Please note the agenda is still being finalised.**

If you are interested in presenting Arches work/developments, or want to lead or suggest a group discussion topic, please do so by using our [call for proposals Google form](https://forms.gle/YQHiwSraLs1pSfi99). We'd love to hear from you!

The call for proposals has a deadline of **10/04**, so even if you’re unsure about the topic or your availability, please let us know what you’re thinking as soon as possible!

We have a range of possible formats, such as a 25 minute presentation + 5 minute Q&A, 10 minute lightning talks, and any form of discussion (open to the group, led by yourself or a nominee, interactive with examples/demo etc). Additionally, we are open to being flexible. If, for example, you have a number of small pieces of work/updates, we would recommend submitting multiple lightning talks which can be either spread out or chained back-to-back to form one larger presentation slot. Lightning talks may also lead into a natural discussion.

<br>

Though the agenda is still being finalised, below is a selection of the topics and themes that the developer meeting will cover. 

Talks:
* Arches Lingo
* Arches 8.1
* QGIS Plugin
* Zod and JS Schema Validation
* Linked Data, CSV Import/Export and Bulk Data Merges
* Rule-based Display Descriptors
* Updates to the Arches CIIM Integration
* Arches Governance 

Discussions:
* Significant discussions around the modularisation of Arches, the boundaries of core Arches and apps, lessons learned from existing applications, and the impact on the direction of Arches development.
* Arches graph migrations, mutations and transformations.
