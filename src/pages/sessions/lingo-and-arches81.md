A presentation of what is in Lingo 1.0 and how it takes advantages of the latest features in Arches 8.1 and related applications, as well as a summary of lessons learned and what the future holds for feature development.  Intended for developers and implementers alike, this will also be an invitation for greater engagement with the project from both groups given the 1.0 release as well as an opportunity to learn from our experience developing Lingo.
