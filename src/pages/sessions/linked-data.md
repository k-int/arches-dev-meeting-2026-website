Getty Provenance Index is now in Production and users have started exercising editorial capabilities of Arches out of the box. There have been challenges in working with Linked Open Data and we have been steadily working on Arches enhancements to provide a better user experience for our scholars at the Getty Research Institute. These enhancements have been contributed to the Arches platform and will be available for use by the larger cultural heritage community.
