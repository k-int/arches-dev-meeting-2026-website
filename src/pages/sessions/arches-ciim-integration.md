A hands-on look at the new Arches integration in CIIM, exploring the challenges and benefits of an 'Extract, Load, Transform' approach.

This approach allows users to extract Arches resources and iteratively transform them with flexibility and without code changes. This enables users to create a unified view of resources across diverging models, present them in alternative output formats (IIIF, Linked Art, MARC, and more), provide more flexible search and data visualisation, and integrate with other back-office systems.
