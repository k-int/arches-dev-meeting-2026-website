Organisations such as those maintaining Historic Environment Records (HERs) need timely and reliable data about changes made to their databases, to meet various auditing and reporting requirements. While Arches automatically populates an edit log table, front-end access to this data is restricted to the 100 most recent edits, with few options to search or filter the results.

In response to requests from multiple customers for more comprehensive access, Knowledge Integration has developed a prototype Arches application, written in Vue, and compatible with Arches 7.6. This presentation provides an overview of the app, discusses the pros and cons of the chosen approach, and explores potential future developments.
