This presentation will cover the review the new editing capability in Modular Reports and touch on some of the Arches extensions that support it (eg: component lab and querysets).

I'll also demonstrate how the new advanced search and data aggregation logic can be used to power dashboards in Arches.  We'll go over some live examples and review the capabilities that the search aggregation logic allows for.  

I think developers and users alike will be interested in both of these topics and at least as far as the Modular reports extension goes, will be an example of where editing and reporting might be going in the future.
